The Challenge

Spokefest of Spokane hired me for a 6-month social media promotion. The challenge-sf was to promote the day of the bike ride on social media platforms so that we may increase the number of new and returning registrants.

Through research, I gathered that the most sizable return came from relatable imagery which users were not likely to scroll past without first looking over.

the solution

Using this method, I could generate interest and maximize involvement with spokefest during community events prior to the day of the ride. Creating frequent incentive to register for spokefest would also motivate a constant flow of new registrants.

I kept a constant stream of imagery to serve as reminder for the upcoming ride and effectively install this year's theme, "Bring a Buddy".

The Result

All efforts to increase registrations were successful, Spokefest 2015 had many more returning riders than in years past. I believe this was a result of increased awareness of incentives to register.
